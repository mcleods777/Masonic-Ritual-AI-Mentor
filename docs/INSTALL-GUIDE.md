# Masonic Ritual Mentor — Install Guide

Brother,

This guide walks you through installing Masonic Ritual Mentor on your phone
and your computer so you can practice your ritual work from memory anywhere.
The app is not in the App Store or Google Play — we install it directly from
the web, which is faster, more private, and entirely under our lodge's control.

The whole process takes about two minutes per device.

---

## Before you start — what you'll need

1. **Your lodge email address.** This is the email the pilot lead has added to
   the approved list. If you're not sure whether you're on the list, ask the
   brother who sent you this guide.
2. **Your encrypted ritual file (the `.mram` file).** You should have received
   this separately, either hand-delivered on a USB stick, by direct message,
   or through whatever channel your lodge uses. It is not distributed through
   this app.
3. **The password for your ritual file.** Memorize it. Do not write it down
   next to the file.
4. **The pilot URL.** The brother who sent you this guide will share the
   current URL. Keep it private to the pilot group.

---

## Part 1 — Sign in (do this first on every device)

The sign-in works the same on every device: type your email, click the link
you receive, and you're in.

1. Open the pilot URL in your web browser.
2. You will see a page that says **Masonic Ritual Mentor — Sign in to the pilot.**
3. Type your lodge email in the box and tap **SEND SIGN-IN LINK**.
4. Check your email inbox. Within a minute or so, you should receive a message
   with a subject line like **"Your sign-in link."** If you do not see it,
   check your spam folder.
5. Open the email and tap the amber **Sign in** button inside it. This opens
   the app with you signed in.
6. You are now on the lodge landing page (a dark page with a glowing blue
   sphere of Masonic symbols). Tap **ENTER THE LODGE**.

The sign-in remembers you for 30 days on this device. You will not need to do
this every time you open the app.

The sign-in link inside the email is only good for 10 minutes. If you wait
longer than that, just request a new link.

## Part 2 — Install as an app (recommended but optional)

Once you are signed in, you can install the site as an app on your phone or
computer. Installing means you get an icon on your home screen or app drawer,
and the site opens in its own window like a native app — no browser address
bar, no distractions, same feel as any other app.

You do not have to install — you can always just use the site in your browser.
But on the phone especially, installing is nicer.

### On iPhone (Safari)

> Works on iPhone and iPad. Must be Safari — the install option does not appear
> in Chrome for iPhone or other browsers.

1. Make sure you are signed in (Part 1) and on the app's landing page.
2. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of Safari. The Share button looks
   like a square with an arrow pointing up out of it. On a phone it is at
   the bottom center; on an iPad it is at the top.
3. In the menu that appears, scroll down until you see **Add to Home Screen**.
   Tap it.
4. Confirm the name **Ritual Mentor** (or change it if you like), then tap
   **Add** in the top right.
5. Go back to your Home Screen. You will see a new icon called **Ritual Mentor**.
   Tap it to open the app in its own window.

### On Android (Chrome or Edge)

1. Make sure you are signed in (Part 1) and on the app's landing page.
2. Tap the **⋮** (three vertical dots) menu in the top right of Chrome.

5. Your phone will place a new icon in your app drawer. Long-press it and

### On Desktop — Microsoft Edge

1. Make sure you are signed in (Part 1) and on the app's landing page.
2. Click the **⋯** (three horizontal dots) menu at the very top right corner
   of the Edge window.
3. Hover over **Apps**.
4. Click **Install this site as an app**.
5. Confirm the name and click **Install**.
6. The app opens in its own window and an icon is added to your Start menu
   (Windows) or Applications (Mac).

### On Desktop — Google Chrome

1. Make sure you are signed in (Part 1) and on the app's landing page.
2. Look at the right end of the address bar (where the URL is displayed).
3. If you see a small icon that looks like a monitor with a downward arrow,
   click it. A popup appears asking **Install Masonic Ritual Mentor?** —
   click **Install**.
4. If you do not see that icon, click the **⋮** (three vertical dots) menu
   at the top right of Chrome, hover over **Cast, save, and share**, then
   click **Install page as app** (wording varies by Chrome version).

1. Make sure you are signed in (Part 1) and on the app's landing page.
2. In the top menu bar, click **File → Add to Dock**.
3. Confirm the name and click **Add**.
4. The app icon appears in your Dock and can be launched from there or from
   Launchpad.

## Part 3 — Load your ritual

This happens once per device, after you sign in.

1. In the app, navigate to the **Upload** screen.
2. Select your encrypted `.mram` file from wherever you saved it.
3. When prompted, enter the password. The file decrypts on your device — it
   is never sent to any server.
4. You are now on the **Practice** screen. Select a role, press the microphone
   button, and begin.

Your ritual file stays on this device. If you want to use the app on a second
device, you load the `.mram` file there too.

---

## Privacy — how the app handles the work

- Your ritual file is encrypted. Without the password, it cannot be read,
  even by someone who steals the file.
- Decryption happens in your browser, on your device. The lodge's server
  never sees the decrypted ritual.
- The app never stores your ritual on any server, ever.
- When the app reads lines aloud, or when you speak a line and the app
  evaluates it, small pieces of text and audio are sent briefly to voice
  and AI services (Voxtral, Google, Deepgram, Groq) for processing. These
  services have no-retention policies — they do not save or train on the
  data.
- If you prefer maximum privacy, the app will eventually include a local-only
  mode that uses only your device's built-in voice features and no cloud
  services at all.

## Troubleshooting

**I didn't get the sign-in email.**

- Wait two minutes, then check spam and promotions folders.
- Make sure you typed your email correctly. Request another link if not.
- If you use iCloud Private Relay on iPhone, try disabling it for the pilot
  or use your real email address (not a `@privaterelay.appleid.com` one).
- If you've waited five minutes and nothing arrived, contact the brother
  who sent you this guide — your email may not be on the approved list yet.

**The sign-in link takes me to a page that says "invalid link."**

- Sign-in links expire in 10 minutes. Request a new one.
- Each link is tied to a specific web address. If you request the link on
  one device and open it on another, make sure both devices are on the same
  pilot URL.

**I can see the app but the "Install" option is missing.**

- Some browser versions don't offer the install option even when the app
  supports it. The app works fine in a browser tab — installation is optional.
- Try the alternate path listed for your browser above.
- On desktop, bookmark the app and make the bookmark part of your bookmarks
  bar. That gets most of the benefit.

**The ritual file won't decrypt.**

- Check the password carefully. It is case-sensitive.
- Make sure you are opening an `.mram` file from our lodge, not an old or
  corrupted copy. If the brother who sent it can re-send, start fresh.

**The AI voice or feedback isn't working.**

- Try a different voice engine from the dropdown at the top of the rehearsal
  screen. If one cloud service is temporarily down, others usually work.
- If none of the cloud engines are available, fall back to the **Browser**
  voice. It's less natural but always works.
